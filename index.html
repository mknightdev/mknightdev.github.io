Mini Jam #89: Pumpkin

Headless Hollow
About

First official game jam I took part in with my housemate; Mini Jam #89: Pumpkin. We had 72 hours to create a game and the limitation was that we had to base our game off of a myth or legend, so we decided to do the headless horseman. I primarily did the programming, with some 3D modelling where needed.

Most models scene in the game were from third-party assets, as we wanted to focus on giving us enough time to execute the idea and get used to the time pressure rather than creating everything from scratch. Future game jams will be from scratch to further develop skills.

3D Model Viewer
About

In this assignment, we had the choice to develop a 3D game, tool or tech demo. I decided to go with creating a tool and developed a 3D model viewer to show custom models and viewing them with the shaders provided with the tool. These shaders ranged from specular, diffuse, ambient and no lighting to give a variety of environments for the model.
