Bodyheat

About

Bodyheat was created for the Digital Technologies unit in first-year using Construct 2. I decided to create a puzzle-platformer game that contained a few varied levels, introducing new features and aspects of the game. The aim of the game is to heat yourself up, or cool yourself down to progress through the levels by interacting with the fans and 'drains'.

This is a quote from my tutor in the feedback provided:

"Body Heat is a really interesting puzzle-platformer that has some nice and original mechanics implemented in it. The concept and the fact that you have made all the art means I can go high on the originality score here. Well done."
